#1. Prototype

I planned two products, iPhone and iPad case, reflecting my experience of purchasing a cell phone and tablet first than anything else as soon as I became a college student. The overall design borrows the receipt design, which serves as a token of purchase and an archive of memories that evokes small memories of the day.

#2. Process

The fabric and all parts were purchased in the Sinseol-dong market, and the sample production was completed in the market.

#3. Final Products

1. I-Phone Case
Two types: Transparent Jelly & Hard
Two versions: Contains Object itself & No graphic in the middle (Use with Grip-tok)

2. I-Pad Pouch
Material: Tyvek, Toilon, Suede
Feature: Can be easily opened and closed with the zipper on the top, and has a band to insert an apple pencil.

3. Tyvek Eco-Bag
Material: Tyvek, Toilon, Nylon
Feature: Eco-friendly by using tyvek material and contains buffer materials in order to protect items inside the bag.
